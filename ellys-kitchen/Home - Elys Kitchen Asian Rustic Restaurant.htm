Welcome to

Ely's Kitchen

Bali is one of those, as the island offers the richness of its distinctive flavors, whether the authentic local culinary or contemporary twist. For those foodies who are tempted to try it, Ely’s Kitchen at Adiwana Unagi Suites is the perfect choice to indulge your senses. Discover a wide array of international delicacies with specializing on Asian cuisines meticulously crafted by the skillful Ely’s Kitchen culinary team. Ely’s Kitchen features a casual semi outdoor dining concept as the restaurant is comfortably accessed as located adjacent to the resort’s lobby. The rustic vibes match with Ubud’s charming personality and the combination of the two make your dining experience top-notch.

Menu

None of our dishes are deep-fried and everything is freshly made to order. This ensures that the flavours of our herbs and vegetables, delivered within 24 hours from local farms, remain as fresh as the day they were picked!
